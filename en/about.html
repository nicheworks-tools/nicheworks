About NicheWorks

NicheWorks is a personally operated site that publishes small, practical web pages for reference and everyday work.

The current main published page is the Old Kanji Reference, a searchable reference for comparing old Japanese kanji forms with modern equivalents. It is intended for checking names, place names, older books, signs, historical documents, and family records.

Operating policy

  • Only reviewed pages are linked from the main navigation during the review period.
  • Usage notes, limitations, contact information, and privacy information are kept visible.
  • Ads, analytics, cookies, and data handling are explained in the Privacy Policy.
  • Unfinished or under-review tools are kept out of the main review path.
