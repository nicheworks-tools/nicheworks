Contact

Thank you for your interest in NicheWorks. You can use the contact information below for questions, feedback, improvement requests, or reporting issues.

1. Email

* The email address is protected using a simple obfuscation script to reduce automated spam collection.

2. Purpose of Inquiry

Your message is welcome for topics such as:

  • Questions about how to use specific tools
  • Bug reports or technical issues
  • Feature suggestions
  • Corrections for data or documentation
  • General feedback or comments

3. Handling of Provided Information

Any information you provide via email will be used solely for:

  • Replying to your inquiry
  • Improving the quality of tools and services

Your information will never be shared with third parties unless legally required.

4. Notes Before Contacting

  • NicheWorks is maintained by an individual developer.
  • Replies may take some time depending on the complexity of the inquiry.
  • Questions about unreleased tools may not be answered.
