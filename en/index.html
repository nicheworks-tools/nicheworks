About

NicheWorks is a collection of small, quiet utilities designed to solve boring but recurring problems. No heavy dashboards, no accounts, no unnecessary data collection â€” just open, use, and close.

Tools

  • ManualFinder
    Link collection to official manual / documentation pages for consumer electronics and more. (Japanese UI)
  • TrashNavi
    Links to official municipal garbage sorting / calendar pages in Japan. (Japanese UI)
  • LogFormatter
    Browser-only formatter for Nginx / HTTP access logs. No upload, runs locally.
  • Rename Wizard
    Filename normalization helper. Preview the new names in the browser, then copy & paste safely.
  • Contract Cleaner
    Highlights potentially risky phrases in contract text for quick manual review. Runs in the browser.
  • EXIF Cleaner Mini
    Tiny EXIF remover for image files. Everything is processed in your browser â€” no image upload to the server.
  • Image Compression Inspector
    Roughly checks compression level / quality and suggests reasonable formats among PNG / JPEG / WebP.
  • LineBreak Doctor
    Fixes line breaks for SNS posts (X, Instagram, LINE, etc.) before you publish.
  • Redirect Unwrapper
    Paste shortened or redirect-heavy URLs to see the final destination URL before opening it.
  • URL Title Collector
    Takes a list of URLs and fetches each page title so you can copy them into notes or spreadsheets.
