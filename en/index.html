Tool Collection

Text & Document Tools

Lightweight utilities for text processing, document cleaning, and log formatting.

  • ManualFinder (Official Manuals Directory)
    A curated index of official manuals for home appliances, cameras, gadgets, and more.
  • TrashNavi (Waste Sorting Directory)
    Links to local government waste-sorting guides and pickup calendars.
  • LogFormatter
    Beautify Nginx / HTTP access logs locally in your browser.
  • Rename Wizard
    Clean and batch-rename filenames with preview.
  • Contract Cleaner
    Highlight risky terms in contract text.
  • EXIF Cleaner Mini
    Remove EXIF metadata from images (local processing).
  • LineBreak Doctor
    Fix broken line breaks for SNS posts.
  • URL Title Collector
    Fetch titles from multiple URLs at once.
  • Message Generator
    Generate short message templates.
