Privacy Policy

NicheWorks provides small browser-based web tools. This policy explains how tool input data, advertisements, analytics, cookies, and contact information are handled.

1. Tool input data

Many tools are designed to run in the browser. Text, images, PDFs, CSV files, and other user inputs are generally not stored on NicheWorks servers. Some individual tools may use external APIs; when they do, the relevant page explains the required communication.

2. Analytics

NicheWorks may use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand site usage and improve the tools. These services may collect information such as visited pages, access times, device and browser information, and approximate region information.

Analytics are used for site improvement and troubleshooting. They are not intended to store the actual text, image, PDF, CSV, or other file contents entered into the tools.

3. Advertising

NicheWorks may display advertisements through Google AdSense to support operation and maintenance. Google AdSense may use cookies or similar technologies to show ads based on user interests or browsing context.

For details about how Google uses advertising data, see Google's advertising technologies policy.

4. Cookie controls

You can disable or delete cookies through your browser settings. Doing so may affect some advertising or analytics behavior.

5. Contact information

Information voluntarily sent by email or other contact methods is used only for replies, troubleshooting, and improving the tools.

6. Disclaimer

NicheWorks tools are general-purpose aids. Accuracy, completeness, and suitability for a specific purpose are not guaranteed. For important decisions, verify information with official sources or qualified professionals.

7. Updates

This policy may be updated as needed. Significant changes will be posted on this page.

For inquiries, please visit the Contact page.
