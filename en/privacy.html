1. About This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how NicheWorks handles access logs, analytics, cookies, and user-submitted information when you use the website.

2. Access Logs

Standard access logs may be recorded by the hosting provider. These logs typically include:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Referrer URL
  • Date and time of access

These logs are used solely for operational monitoring and troubleshooting.

NicheWorks uses lightweight analytics (Cloudflare Web Analytics) to understand aggregated usage patterns. Personally identifiable information is not collected.

4. Cookies

NicheWorks does not issue tracking cookies for its own purposes.
