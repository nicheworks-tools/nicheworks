Usage (EN)

Paste contract text and extract potentially risky clause patterns (this is not legal advice).

  • Switch JP/EN mode (pattern sets)
  • Select contract type (v1 focuses on Services)
  • Click Analyze to see Findings and Risk level
  • Markdown preview is free; downloads are Pro

Privacy: Analysis runs in your browser; analytics/ads scripts may load. Avoid sensitive data.
