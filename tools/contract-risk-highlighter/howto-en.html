Contract red flags checklist: questions to confirm (UK-oriented)

Goal: reduce misses. Not legal advice. Use patterns + questions to confirm critical points.

The fastest review order

  1. Governing law / jurisdiction
  2. Limitation of liability
  3. Indemnity
  4. Termination
  5. IP assignment / licence

Copy-paste question templates

Limitation of liability

  • What is the liability cap and what is it based on (fees paid / contract value)?
  • Are indirect/consequential losses excluded symmetrically for both parties?

Indemnity

  • What triggers the indemnity and how broad is the scope?
  • Are defence costs included? Any limitations?

Termination

  • Is termination for convenience allowed for both parties? What notice period applies?
  • What happens to fees/work-in-progress on termination?
