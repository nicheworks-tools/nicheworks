Purchase flow

  1. Purchase through the common Payment Link.
  2. Stripe returns to /pro/unlock/?session_id={CHECKOUT_SESSION_ID}.
  3. /assets/nw-pro.js checks /api/pro/status.
  4. The server-side source of truth is D1 pro_entitlements with entitlement nicheworks_pro.
  5. This browser stores the active helper state, then Motion Atlas reads NWPro.getLocalStatus().

After purchase, NicheWorks Pro is enabled in this browser. It usually remains active after closing the tab or browser. You may need to unlock again on another device, another browser, private mode, or after clearing site data.

Limitations and shared foundation

  • Local storage is only a helper state; D1 pro_entitlements is the server-side source of truth.
  • Motion Atlas uses the common client /assets/nw-pro.js and common entitlement nicheworks_pro.
  • Motion Atlas does not create a separate Stripe product, per-tool webhook, or per-tool D1 store.
  • Free motion viewing, searching, demos, reduced-motion checks, and 2-motion compare are not moved behind Pro.
