Open the tool 日本語ガイド

Ranks: SAFE / CAUTION / RISK with a short note.

Use this when

  • You want a quick “paste → scan” ingredient safety hint
  • You have Japanese ingredient labels and need English mapping
  • You can’t copy text and want to OCR from a photo
  • You want to spot common irritants before buying/using

Keywords: ingredient safety checker, INCI analyzer, J-beauty ingredient translator, OCR ingredients

How to use

  1. TAB 1: paste ingredients → hit Check Safety
  2. TAB 2: paste Japanese ingredients (or upload a photo for OCR) → Translate & Check Safety
  3. Review the SAFE/CAUTION/RISK results and the Unknown list

If Japanese is detected in TAB 1, the tool may suggest switching to TAB 2 (if implemented).

Notes (limits)

  • This is a dictionary-based hint, not medical advice.
  • Reactions depend on concentration, usage, and individual sensitivity.
  • OCR quality depends on the photo; unreadable items can become Unknown.
  • If you see Unknown, try simplifying the name (remove extra symbols) and retry.
