Future direction

Vibe Lexicon Pro (planned)

Pro is a direction for deeper decision support, not a fake-live sales page. The goal is to expand the free foundation for teams that need stronger comparison depth and reusable language standards across ongoing product work.

Intended Pro value

Deeper comparison

Go beyond basic side-by-side checks with stronger differentiation signals when terms are close but outcomes differ.

Richer breakdown

Explain tone, intent, and implementation tradeoffs in more depth so teams can choose language deliberately.

Reusable saved sets

Support repeated workflows where teams keep curated wording sets for specific product surfaces and quality standards.

Why deeper capability matters

Single-screen edits are usually simple. Multi-screen updates are not. CTA clarity, clutter reduction, mobile readability, and form clarity often need consistent choices across many surfaces. Pro direction focuses on that repeated decision load.

How it relates to free

Free remains genuinely useful and public: search, detail, basic compare, and short prompt copy stay practical on their own.

Current status (honest note)

Payment and paid controls are not live at this time. There is no hidden checkout and no fake locked UI being presented as active.

Roadmap updates will be published here only when concrete functionality is ready for real use.
