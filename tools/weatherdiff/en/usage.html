How to use WeatherDiff

Weather forecast comparison tool

Overview

WeatherDiff lines up Open-Meteo and MET Norway forecasts so you can quickly see differences in temperature, precipitation, and wind. It runs in your browser, no sign-up required.

Steps

  1. Type a place name in the search box or tap "Use my location" to set your point.
  2. Press "Compare" to load today and tomorrow forecasts from Open-Meteo and MET Norway.
  3. Open "Forecast difference (comparison)" to view gaps in high/low temperature, precipitation, and wind. Only the high temperature is highly reliable.

Notes

  • Minimum temperature and precipitation values may vary because they rely on estimates.
  • Always confirm official weather and disaster information for your area.
  • Using location search requires browser permission for geolocation.
