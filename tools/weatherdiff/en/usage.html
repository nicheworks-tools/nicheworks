How to use WeatherDiff

Weather forecast comparison tool

Overview

WeatherDiff compares forecasts from Open-Meteo and MET Norway so you can quickly see differences in temperature, precipitation, and wind. It runs in your browser and is free to use.

Steps

  1. Type a place name in the search box or tap "Use my location" to set your point.
  2. Select "Compare" to load today and tomorrow forecasts from both APIs.
  3. Check "Forecast difference (comparison)" to view gaps in high/low temperature, precipitation, and wind. Treat values other than high temperature as reference only.

Notes

  • Minimum temperature and precipitation values may vary because they rely on estimates.
  • Always confirm official weather and disaster information for your area.
  • Using location search requires browser permission for geolocation.
