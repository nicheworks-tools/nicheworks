30-second steps

  1. Open the tool
  2. Paste contract text or terms
  3. Click “Analyze” to see category summaries and highlights
  4. Review conditions, exceptions, and deadlines in context

What it can / can’t do

  • Highlights common risk words by category using a local dictionary.
  • It does not judge legality or provide legal advice.
  • Paraphrases not in the dictionary may be missed.

Note: This is not legal advice.

Privacy

All processing happens in your browser. Text is not uploaded.

Commonly flagged examples

  • Liability caps, disclaimers, limitation of liability
  • Termination, cancellation, penalties
  • Intellectual property ownership and deliverables
  • Confidentiality scope and disclosure exceptions
  • Governing law and jurisdiction
