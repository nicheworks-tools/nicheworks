URL Title Collector

Fetch page titles from multiple URLs and copy them as CSV/TSV

How to use

Paste one URL per line to fetch each page's <title>. You can copy the results as CSV or TSV.

Important notice

This tool sends entered URLs to a NicheWorks Worker to fetch page titles. Do not enter private URLs, internal URLs, login-only pages, or URLs containing personal information.

For short links or suspicious URLs, check them with Redirect Unwrapper first.

Only http / https URLs are processed. Invalid or duplicate URLs are not sent to the Worker and are shown with a skip reason.

FAQ

Are entered URLs sent anywhere?

Yes. Entered URLs are sent to a NicheWorks Worker so it can fetch the page title.

Can I enter internal or login-only URLs?

No. Do not enter private, internal, login-only, or sensitive URLs.

Can fetches still fail?

Yes. The Worker may fail when the target site blocks access, times out, or returns a non-HTML response.

Does this tool unwrap short URLs?

This tool is for title fetching. Use Redirect Unwrapper to inspect short links or tracking redirects.
