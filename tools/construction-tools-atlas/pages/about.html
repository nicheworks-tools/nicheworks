EN

Construction Tools & Slang Atlas is a bilingual (EN/JA) reference for essential construction field terms, tools, and work processes. It is designed for quick lookup with short, practical descriptions.

If a term is missing or inaccurate, please treat it as “work in progress” and prioritize official manuals and on-site rules.
