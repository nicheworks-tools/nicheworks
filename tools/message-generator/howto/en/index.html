What it does

  • Creates short templates: requests, thanks, follow-ups, apologies, scheduling
  • Helps adjust tone (polite / neutral / casual — depending on the tool)
  • Copy-ready output you can tweak quickly
  • Browser-only, no storage

How to use (3 steps)

  1. Select a purpose (request/thanks/decline/follow-up…)
  2. Enter key details (what, when, deadline, context)
  3. Copy the output and edit names/dates before sending

Tip: always double-check names and dates to avoid mistakes.

Notes (limits)

  • Appropriate tone depends on your relationship and workplace culture.
  • For sensitive/legal situations, don’t send without reviewing.
  • Don’t enter personal or confidential data.
