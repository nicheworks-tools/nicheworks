How to Use (30 sec)

Quick guide to generate privacy snippets for analytics.

  1. Enter your site name, optional URL, and contact email.
  2. Select the analytics providers you use.
  3. Choose the output language and click “Generate”.
  4. Copy from the three tones or download a TXT file.

When to edit

  • If your cookie or tracking setup is custom.
  • If laws require additional disclosures or consent.
  • If you need details on data sharing or cross-border transfers.
