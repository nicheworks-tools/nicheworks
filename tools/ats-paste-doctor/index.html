FAQ

Why do line breaks or bullets break in ATS forms?
Many ATS/web forms strip formatting or collapse whitespace. This tool normalizes text to safer plain text.
Is my text uploaded or saved?
No. Processing runs locally in your browser; only Pro status may be stored locally.
What do ‚ÄúATS-safe / Keep line breaks / Clean‚Äù mean?
ATS-safe flattens bullets/paragraphs, Keep line breaks preserves line structure, Clean removes invisible/control characters.
Why do I see warnings like invisible chars or mixed newlines?
Those hidden characters can cause paste glitches. The warnings flag risky patterns so you can fix them.
How do I unlock Pro ($2.99) and what persists?
Complete the payment, then revisit with ?pro=1. Pro status is saved on this device only.
