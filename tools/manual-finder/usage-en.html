How to Use ManualFinder

ManualFinder helps you reach official manufacturer manual pages and search there by model number, product name, or series.

What this tool does

  • Find official manual pages from major brands such as Apple, Sony, Panasonic, Canon, Epson, Brother, HP, Dell and Lenovo.
  • Filter the directory by brand, category, or product type.
  • Start from official manufacturer pages instead of unofficial PDF mirrors.

Steps

  1. Enter a brand, category, or product type.
  2. Select a category if needed.
  3. Open “Official manuals” or “Support portal” from the result card.
  4. Search the destination site by model number, product name, or series.

Supported / unsupported

  • Supported: links to official manual and support pages.
  • Unsupported: hosting PDFs, repair advice, or guaranteeing manufacturer search results.

FAQ

Does this site host PDFs?
No. Please confirm and download manuals on the destination official site.

What if I do not know the model number?
Check the product label, box, warranty card, invoice, or order history.

Disclaimer

We do not guarantee the accuracy of listings, product information, or destination pages. Always confirm the latest details on official manufacturer websites.
