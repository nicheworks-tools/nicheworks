What this tool does

  • Regenerates JPEG, PNG, and WebP images locally in your browser.
  • Creates a new image file that does not carry over most EXIF metadata.
  • Does not upload your image to a server.

How to use

  1. Open the tool and choose a JPEG, PNG, or WebP image.
  2. Check the quick EXIF result. It is not a full GPS or metadata analysis.
  3. Choose the output format and JPEG quality if needed.
  4. Click the clean and download button.
  5. Share the saved file with -cleaned in its filename.

Limits and safety notes

  • This does not guarantee removal of every app-specific metadata field or file-level trace.
  • It does not hide names, faces, addresses, order numbers, or other visible information inside the image.
  • HEIC, TIFF, RAW, PDF, and video files are not supported.
  • The original file is not changed. Delete it yourself if needed.
