What it does

  • Convert old-form kanji (Kyūjitai) → modern kanji (Shinjitai)
  • Optional reverse: modern → old (if supported)
  • Useful for names, places, old documents, and normalization
  • Fully local processing, no storage

Use this when

  • You need a modern spelling for old kanji in forms or documents
  • You want searchable text from scanned/old materials
  • You want consistent kanji forms for indexing

Keywords: kyujitai to shinjitai, old kanji converter, Japanese kanji normalization

How to use

  1. Paste your text
  2. Select direction (old→modern / modern→old)
  3. Copy the output

Tip: for proper nouns, verify against the source document.

Notes (limits)

  • Rare variants not in the dictionary may not convert.
  • Some characters are context-dependent (names/places).
  • For official procedures, always prioritize the original/official spelling.
