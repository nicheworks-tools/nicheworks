Moving Checklist: Guide

Turn “I don’t know what to do first” into a date-ordered checklist.

What it does

  • Generates a timeline checklist from 7 days before to moving day
  • Adjusts items based on your conditions (depends on available options)
  • Check-off status is saved locally on your device
  • Print/PDF-friendly layout
Open the tool 日本語ガイド

How to use

  1. Enter your moving date
  2. Select conditions (solo/family, housing type, etc.)
  3. Check off tasks as you go (print or save as PDF if needed)

Tip: the fewer tasks left for moving day, the easier everything gets.

Notes (limits)

  • Required procedures vary by country/city and by contract—confirm official sources.
  • Local check-off data may not sync across devices.
  • Clearing browser data can remove saved checkmarks.
