How to use

Moving Checklist Generator — steps & notes.

Back to tool

1. What it does

Generates a timeline checklist (7 days before → moving day) based on your situation.

2. Steps

  1. Select your moving date.
  2. Choose Solo/Family and Rental/Owned.
  3. Click “Generate checklist”.
  4. Check completed items (saved locally in this browser).
  5. Use “Print / Save as PDF” if needed.

3. Supported / Unsupported

  • Supported: local saving, print/PDF, JP/EN switch
  • Unsupported: cloud sync, share links, perfect coverage of all situations

4. Errors

You must select a moving date to generate a checklist.

5. Output format

Grouped by day blocks with checkable tasks.

6. FAQ

Checks won’t sync across devices and may be cleared if you delete browser data.
